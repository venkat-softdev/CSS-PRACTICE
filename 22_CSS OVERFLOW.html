Key properties of Overflow:

  • Overflow: Sets the behaviour for both horizontal and vertical overflow (eg; 'auto' 'hidden' 'scroll' 'visible')
  • overflow-x: contrlos the overflow on the horizontal axis.
  • overfllow-y: controls the overflow on the vertical axis.

value of overflow properties:

  • visible: default value, content is not clipped,and may be rendered outside the box.
  • hidden: content is clipped ,and the rest is hidden.
  • scroll: content is clipped ,but scrolling is always visible.
  • auto: content is clipped,and a scrollbar is visible only if neccesarry.
